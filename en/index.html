Located in the heart of the Minho surrounded by magnificent landscapes!

The Pensão Parque is located in Caldelas Thermal Spa, with a privileged location right in front of the thermal complex.

Located 10 miles from Braga and 22 miles from Gerês, Pensão Parque is in a privileged place for those who want to restore their energies, relax and enjoy an unchanged nature.

Its surroundings allow for an unforgettable relaxation.

Relax with a walk surrounded by nature through the Alvito pedestrian path.

You can also relax in Municipal swimming pool of Caldelas which a high quality tourist equipment, ready to provide unforgettable moments of leisure.

Come and visit Caldelas Thermal Spa, its gardens, the river Alvito, S. Pedro de Fins hill, Ponte de Rodas and all its beauty.

Come and visit Pensão Parque and get to know nature while enjoying a quiet and familiar environment.

Little curiosity

The name of Caldelas (Caldellas in ancient spelling), is all of Latin origin and its meaning relates directly to the mineral-medicinal springs. Thus, etymologically, the word "Calldelas" is a word derived from the Latin Latin term "calda" (meaning hot water) with the diminutive suffix "ella" also Latin (which joins the idea of smallness), the plural being used to refer to two springs. Thus "Caldellas" literally means "small warm waters", being the name of the town naturally linked to the period of Romanization referred to above, existing written records of this name since the XIII century. Caldelas, with only one, is the modern spelling, adopted in the first quarter of this century.
